Cranberry Salad

Photo from Allrecipes.com by Julia Hartbeck

This cranberry salad is a fabulous blend of fresh cranberries, walnuts, orange, crushed pineapple, and gelatin.

This festive and fruity cranberry salad recipe will be the star of your holiday dinner table.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cranberries
  • 1 large orange, cut into small pieces
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 cup finely chopped walnuts
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1 (3 ounce) package cranberry (or raspberry) flavored Jell-O mix
  • 2 cups hot water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place cranberries, orange (including rind), and sugar in a food processor. Process together until the cranberries and orange are roughly ground and well combined with sugar.
  3. Transfer to a bowl. Stir in celery, walnuts, and pineapple until thoroughly mixed.
  4. Place gelating in a bowl and pour hot water over top (don't let stand). Whisk together to dissolve.
  5. Pour gelatin over the cranberry mixture. Stir to combine.
  6. Pour into the mold of your choice and chill until you are ready to serve.
